# Aeonmi Quantum Workflow Logo Design

## Logo Concept: Æ + Q.U.B.E. Quantum Symbol

### Primary Logo Elements:
- **Æ** (Aeonmi) symbol in quantum blue (#00FFFF)
- **Q.U.B.E.** acronym with quantum glyphs
- **Quantum circuit** background pattern
- **Hadamard gate** (H) symbol integrated
- **Superposition waves** emanating from center

### Color Scheme:
- **Primary**: #00FFFF (Quantum Cyan)
- **Secondary**: #FF6B00 (Quantum Orange)
- **Background**: #0A0A0A (Dark Quantum Void)
- **Accent**: #00FF00 (Quantum Green)
